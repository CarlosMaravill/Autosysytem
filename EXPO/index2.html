WHAT IS AUTOSYSTEM?

The purpose of the project is to be the area's trust tool straightening and painting, since being a work area with a lack of options or possibilities administrative data, records or other information is often lost. The application of our system will allow the simple and complete management of data according to labor from straightening and painting workshops, taking control over all data received from possible entries to Registration. It has the necessary tools for the program to fulfill the tasks requested by part of the client, in addition to being a much more efficient option than common paperwork, it will also have the security necessary to guarantee total control of this data without risk of loss or alterations.
